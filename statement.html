Artist Statement

Being an artist to me is having the ability to be creative and think "outside the box", while also having fun and enjoying the work created. I hope that my art presented in the portfolio represent that. In the digital art shown int he portfolio it consists of multiple different mediums: glitch art, composite art, sound art, video art, and net art.

A majority of the work shown is a learning process and I think represent my growth within the digital art community. In the work that I have presented it is certain things that are "outside of the box" and displays how the social issues currently in the world.
